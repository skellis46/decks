## applied embodied research

Over five years, I would aim to shift the Centre’s external-facing pipeline — bids, partnerships, communications, knowledge exchange and income development — from being predominantly dance-centred to being more strongly oriented around applied embodied research. REF outputs and core research outputs would remain excellence-led, with dance research continuing as the Centre’s disciplinary foundation and source of authority.
